A Few words about us

AISHI is a crowdfunding platform that helps raise funds for patients, NGOs, and natural calamities. With a mission to make a positive impact in the world, AISHI provides a platform for individuals and communities to come together and support those in need. Whether it's helping to cover medical expenses, supporting a non-profit organization, or donating to disaster relief efforts, AISHI is dedicated to making a difference and bringing hope to those who need it most.
